My story

I'm Vincent, 24 years old, and I will be one of your future java developer colleagues. I used to live in Bierbeek but recently moved to Leuven. I studied civil engineering at KULeuven for 5 years, where I had the chance to try out programming for the first time, which evolved into my love for computer sciences. Sadly I wasn't made for university life and had to look for alternative ways to get myself a job in this sector. After a course at VDAB where I got to hone my workfloor skills and after an interesting job search during interesting times, I had the opportunity to join this diverse group of people, enabling me to grow both personally as well as professionally. Which brings us to the present where I can't wait to get started!
